ABOUT ME

download resume

Hi, my name is Malcolm and I am a Melbourne based Web Designer who believes in creating the best possible IT solutions for my clients.

What can I do for your business?

Web design is no longer just about presenting dry paragraphs of information, it’s about creating an engaging and artistic solution that can be accessed quickly and easily by the widest possible audience.

How Do I do this?

  • By placing a strong emphasis on search engine optimisation (SEO) in regards to visible content, metadata and the coding structure
  • Minimising loading times to maximise end-user satisfaction
  • Optimised coding to increase accessibility to a global audience
  • Screen reader friendly coding
  • Ensuring that older browsers render the website effectively to maximise accessibility.
  • Scalable and robust design that seamlessly adapts to large monitor resolutions or small screen portable devices
  • All graphical design work will be heuristically evaluated and tested to ensure compliance with Nielsen’s Heuristics, Norman’s design principles and Shneiderman’s Golden Rules
